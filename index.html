Antonia has lived in 8 countries, travelled in many more and speaks 4 languages. Aside from her work, she knits, acts in and directs theatre, watches all kind of sport and sails. She has played bass in a pub band, danced Flamenco in Jerez, and done courses on cartooning. She has turned into a front-end web development obsessive. She draws on all these experiences to gain different perspectives on problems and challenges.

Antonia has a Masters from the University of Melbourne, during which she studied creative and critical thinking and wrote her thesis on organisational networks. She has experience in communication, media relations, policy development & coordination and productivity improvement across the public, educational, not-for-profit and private sectors.
